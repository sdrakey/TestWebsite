Find Your Voice – A Free 8 Week Course for Men

Posted JULY 10 2016.

Led by Musical Director Tim Knight Find Your Voice is an free 8 week singing course starting in January 2017. Designed to introduce men of all singing experience (including none!) to singing and covering tone, dynamics, breathing and singing in voice parts you will be guided through the eight weeks by our expert musical team, with an opportunity to perform alongside Leeds Male Voice Choir in March 2017.

Rehearsals will be held every Tuesday from 31 January 2017 starting at 7.30pm. Register.

Join our Musical Team as Trainee Conductor

Posted Oct 21 2016.

We are seeking an enthusiastic and dynamic Trainee Conductor to join our musical team, working with our musical director Tim Knight.

During the twelve month internship, you will have the opportunity to lead sectional rehearsals, deputise for the Musical Director, and conduct smaller performances during the choir’s busy concert schedule. The most recent post holder has used the role to springboard into the role of Musical Director for a large female chorus in West Yorkshire..

With strong musicality and some insight into conducting, you will be keen to develop your experience and you will receive regular support and conducting training with our Musical Director. Strong interpersonal and musical skills, along with the ability to work with a cross range of singers will support you in this role.

About

Formed in 1916 in Middleton Leeds Male Voice Choir have been entertaining audiences for a century. With a proud history and a reputation for innovation, we are an inclusive, vibrant and dynamic choir, with members from all walks of life.
